Welcome to BrighterBeauty™

At the intersection of beauty, science and technology, BrighterBeauty™ creates customized skincare solutions designed to meet the skincare needs of our busy urban consumers. BrighterBeauty’s patented 3A Technology™ supports a proprietary product line.

BrighterBeauty™ provides
Next Generation Retail Experiences

Skincare is no longer just for women, men in China are increasingly confident about skincare and are willing to invest their time and money in skincare routines. Designing effective, efficient and experiential “Next Generation” skincare solutions for both genders of busy urban customers is our goal.

Why We Love This Space

China’s “skintellectuals” are some of the most knowledgeable skincare consumers in the world. Moving at the speed of China, balancing the responsibilities of having a successful career, being a supportive spouse, parent, child and friend is never easy. Our customers look for solutions that deliver results and fit their dynamic schedules.

Individually Designed Programs with Freshly Blended Solutions

Individually customized formulations for the 8-Minute Rejuvenation™ sessions are blended freshly on each visit to one of Shanghai’s BrighterBeauty™ Centers. Through data driven and custom blended solutions, BrighterBeauty™ is committed to help modern urban consumers achieve their individual skincare goals.

Data Driven Decision Making

Visia® Facial Imaging, provides customers with a third-party skin assessment that provides data and insights into current skin status and measures results achieved within the program.
