Explore Our Technology

Active Atomization Application Technology™

Leveraging technology and decades of experience BrighterBeauty’s patented Active Atomization Application Technology™ powers the atomization of active ingredients for even application and rapid absorption. This technology supports a proprietary product line including H.ydration Rejuvenation, B.right Approach, A.geless Generation, and P.ollution Protection with more lines to come.

Active Blend Dispenser

Your serum will be blended fresh for you on each visit based on the custom blend you designed for your treatment package. Customers can include additional ingredients to single treatment sessions to address changes in lifestyle, weather and pollution levels.

Visia® Facial Imaging

Using Visia® Facial Imaging, customers are provided with a third party skin assessment that provides data and insights into current skin status and measures results achieved within the program. The Visia® Scanner measures eight attributes of your skin and compares your results to a global data base of people you are your age, sex and have similar skin tone.

Red Light Therapy Pod

As a benefit to our Diamond level members, BrighterBeauty™ offers the opportunity to enjoy Red Light Therapy sessions as part of an active treatment package. This black technology is popular across the marketplace. BrighterBeauty offers a full body treatment option.
