SAY HELLO TO OUR PARTNERS

BrighterBeauty™ is honored to be the first retail brand in China to have products Leaping Bunny Certified in the pilot program between Cruelty Free International, Fengpu District, Oriental Beauty Valley and Knundsen&CRC.
Leaping Bunny Certification is the leading globally recognized cruelty free certification for cosmetics, personal care, and household products. Certified companies must meet rigorous criteria, including independent checks and on-going audits, before they can display the Leaping Bunny logo. More than 1200 companies globally hold Leaping Bunny Certification.
